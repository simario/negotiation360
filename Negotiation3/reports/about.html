Negotiation 360° was developed by Harvard Business School professor and author Michael Wheeler to help you identify current skills you can build on, and other areas where you can improve. It highlights key negotiation concepts and techniques for creating value, engaging other parties, and learning from experience.

Negotiation 360° has three closely linked features. The Self-assessment feature prompts you to create a personalized profile reflecting your basic negotiation style. It also explains fundamental tensions in negotiation that you must address in formulating strategy.

The Scorecard feature enables you to do an after-action-review of every negotiation you undertake, large or small. It enables you to draw the right lessons from your own on-going experience.

And then, preparing for your next negotiation, always consult the third feature, My best practices. It compiles what you've learned from prior experience so that you can continually enhance your strategic and interpersonal skills. Be sure to consult the Resources section, as well, that points you to other sources of valuable negotiation advice.

To begin, tap Next above and take the short self-assessment test. Then analyze your feedback and review the tips for making better deals.
