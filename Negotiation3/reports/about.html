Everyone negotiates. At work. In their communities. And even at home with their families. Negotiating well means:

  • reaching agreements that might slip through most people's fingers
  • crafting value-creating solutions
  • resolving small differences before they become costly disputes

Successful negotiators are well-rounded. They rely on complementary interpersonal, analytic, and problem-solving skills.

Great negotiatiors also are agile. They adjust their approach to the situation at hand and deal effectively with different bargaining styles.

Negotiation 360° provides you with a personalized profile of your relational and problem-solving skills. It identifies skills that you can build on and other areas where you can improve. It also highlights key negotiation concepts about creating value and engaging other parties.
