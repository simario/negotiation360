Everyone negotiates. At work. In their communities. And even at home with their families. Negotiating well means:

  • reaching agreements that might slip through most people's fingers
  • crafting value-creating solutions
  • resolving small differences before they become costly disputes

Successful negotiators are well-rounded. They rely on complementary interpersonal, analytic, and problem-solving skills.

Great negotiators also are agile. They adjust their approach to the situation at hand and deal effectively with different bargaining styles.

Negotiation 360° was developed by Harvard Business School professor and author Michael Wheeler to help you identify skills you can build on, as well as other areas where you can improve. It highlights key negotiation concepts and techniques for creating value, engaging other parties, and learning from experience.

It has three closely linked features. The Self-assessment feature prompts you to create a personalized profile that reflects your basic negotiation style. It also introduces you fundamental tensions in negotiation that you must address in formulating strategy.

The Scorecard feature enables you to do an after-action-review of every negotiation you undertake, large or small. It enables you to draw the right lessons from your own on-going experience.
