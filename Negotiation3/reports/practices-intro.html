Building a personalized list of best practices will help you:

  • Reach agreements that might otherwise slip through your fingers.
  • Generate (and capture) value through creative problem-solving.
  • Resolve small disputes before they escalate into costly conflicts.

Your success, however, depends on the effort you invest into learning from your experience, in order to polish your negotiation skills. The good news is that thoughtful preparation does not take much time. Remember: you never want to be less prepared than the other negotiator at the table.

This feature on the app allows review what has worked well for you in the past (and what you would do differently in the future). The Negotiation Performance section allows you to track your performance over time. You can also navigate back to the Tips offered in the self-assessment feature.

To take full advantage of the app, periodically review the kinds of negotiations you engage in, how important they are, and how you feel you've performed. Look for general patterns and trends.
