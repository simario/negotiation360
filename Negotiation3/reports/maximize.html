Maximize your share

  • Don't get "anchored." Work from your number. Not theirs.
  • Watch the clock. If the other party is under a deadline, use that to your advantage.
  • Try "post-settlement settlement." After reaching a firm agreement, suggest that you review the terms to see if adjusting the terms might help both parties.
  • Avoid the winner's curse. You may wonder if you could have gotten more. The only way to know for sure is to demand too much and blow up the deal.
