The Program on Negotiation based at the Harvard Law School is another excellent resource

Michael Wheeler's The Art of Negotiation (especially the chapters on openings, critical moments, and closing) closely examines interpersonal dynamics

David Lax and James Sebenius's The Manager as Negotiator (in particular, the chapter on creating and claiming value) provides important analytic insights

Beyond Winning (notably, the chapter on empathy and assertiveness), by Robert Mnookin, Scott Peppet, and Andrew Tulumello illuminates three tensions in negotiation
